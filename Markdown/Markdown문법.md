## 3. 글씨체

```md
**Bold**
*italic*
~~strikethrough~~
> I never dreamed about success, I worked for it.
```
**Bold** <br>
*italic* <br>
~~strikethrough~~ <br>
> I never dreamed about success, I worked for it.
